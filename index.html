A matter of semantics

What's the point?

According to the dictionary, semantics refers to the meaning of a:

  • word
  • phrase
  • sentence
  • text

In the world of HTML, the word semantics means essentially the same thing.

According to an article published by Codepen in January, 2016 called An Overview of HTML5 Semantics,"a semantic element clearly describes its meaning to both the browser and developer."

In my experience, anything that can provide clarity and accessibility is a good thing.
